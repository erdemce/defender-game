Air-Defender

Instructions:

  • You are controlling the air-defender.
  • You can target by mouse and click to fire.
  • Try to destroy all of the air-vehicles.
  • Each air-vehicle has got different health and speed.
  • If you can destroy all of the air-vehicles in the level, the next level starts automatically.
  • If one of the air-vehicles can reach at the bottom, you are destroyed and loose!
